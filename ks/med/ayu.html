AYURVEDIC MEDICINES

Ayurvedic treatment combines products (mainly derived from plants, but

may also include animal, metal, and mineral), diet, exercise, and lifestyle.

where does ayurveda come from?

Ayurvedic theory states that all areas of life impact one's health, so it

follows that the Vedas cover a wide variety of topics, including health and

healthcare techniques, astrology, spirituality, government and politics, art,

and human behavior.
